Tribute to Ecchiro Oda

Eiichiro Oda, the creator of One Piece, is a highly respected figure in the manga industry, known for his storytelling and character development. He's also known for paying homage to other mangaka, like Kohei Horikoshi of My Hero Academia, by redrawing fan art Horikoshi submitted as a young fan.

“A man dies when he is forgotten! ”
